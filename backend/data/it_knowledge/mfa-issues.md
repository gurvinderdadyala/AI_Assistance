# MFA Issues

Multi-factor authentication protects access to company systems.

If you are not receiving MFA prompts:

1. Confirm your phone has internet access and notifications enabled.
2. Open the authenticator app manually and check for pending prompts.
3. Use a backup verification method if one is registered.
4. Restart the phone if push notifications still do not arrive.
5. Try signing in again after two minutes.

If your phone was replaced, lost, or reset, contact the IT helpdesk. The helpdesk must verify your identity before registering a new MFA device.
