# Password Reset

Employees can reset their network password from the self-service password portal.

1. Go to the company password portal from a trusted browser.
2. Select **Forgot password**.
3. Enter your company email address and complete MFA verification.
4. Create a password with at least 14 characters, including uppercase, lowercase, number, and symbol.
5. Wait two minutes before signing in to email, VPN, or workstation services.

If the portal says your account is locked, wait 15 minutes and try again. Contact the IT helpdesk if the account remains locked or you no longer have access to your MFA device.
