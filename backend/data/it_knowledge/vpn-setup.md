# VPN Setup

Use the company VPN when accessing internal systems from outside the office.

1. Install the approved VPN client from the software portal.
2. Open the VPN client and choose the **Corporate Remote Access** profile.
3. Sign in with your company email address and current password.
4. Approve the MFA prompt on your registered device.
5. Confirm the client status shows **Connected** before opening internal apps.

If VPN fails to connect, restart the VPN client, check your internet connection, and verify your password works in the webmail portal. Persistent connection errors should be sent to the IT helpdesk with a screenshot and the time of the failure.
